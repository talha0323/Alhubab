About Us

ALHUBAB is the dream-child of a market visionary Jamshed Hasan a marketing man with over three decades experience and exposure to vital media functions. He has held senior positions in India’s better known media organizations, and has been commended by his peers for his dedication, initiative, applied knowledge and achievements on Public Relations. In the course of his long career, he has come across a wide variety of Senior Executives holding key positions in the industry and commerce, and effectively learned how to cope with their egos.

ALHUBAB is not only a daily newspaper but a MISSION were almost all the section of the society, especially the minorities; contribute a bit as per their capacity to raise the country on top of the GLOBE. It is a real fact that the minorities have scarified thousands of lives, too, to make this country free from the British but have been deprived in many ways like education, social and political status, image …………..etc.

Jamshed Hasan has realised, and you also would agree, that during the last 73 years of our Independence, we have never realised to establish a mouthpiece for the community which is of the community, for the community and byr the community to raise their issues and provide them a platform to resolve their problems and bring them up into the main stream, upgrade and uplift them to such a level so that they may contribute to the national growth and development, and, ALHUBAB is the first step towards this mission….

NOW, Jamshed Hasan has decided to branch of on his own so that the society and its people and the organizations can benefit from the richness of his knowledge and experiences………

Thus ALHUBAB, a very strong Urdu media vehicle (in Urdu language) started with a purpose …

  • Urdu, the mother tongue of the 2nd largest majority of the nation, but neglected.
  • To provide news and views without any fear or bias.
  • Speak in the language of the common man of the country.
  • To reach up to the last needy person and raise their issues through its pages
  • To bring the community in the main stream thus to contribute in the growth, development, prosperity of the nation.
  • To make the market aware the strength of this media and the importance of its readerships.
  • Focus on the development and to upgrade of the society especially the minorities.
  • Lure the youths towards education and provide them opportunities and guidelines through its pages.
  • Provide the youths avenues of jobs.
  • To speak the mind and the language of its readers.
  • ………….and many more.

As “A lone gram can’t bust the oven”, likewise Jamshed Hasan alone cannot succeed in this noble mission without the encouragement, guidance, co-operations and help from like-minded people of the society who, too, want to see the community in the main stream and contribute to the national growth and development…..and it is sure you are the one who is also having the same feelings to whom we can reach for the guidance and discuss the issues of the society as well as the aims and objectives of this mission.
